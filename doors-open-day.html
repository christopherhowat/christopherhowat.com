Glasgow Doors Open Day

Photography Graphic Design Digital Content Festival

About the project

Glasgow Doors Open Day Festival is an annual event opening up the city's most remarkable buildings and spaces to the public, free of charge, for one weekend each September.

I worked on the digital content and graphic design for the event at The Whisky Bond, creating promotional materials for the Glasgow School of Art Archives & Collections, Glasgow Sculpture Studios, and the Alasdair Gray Archive, all resident organisations at the venue.

The visual identity balanced the industrial character of The Whisky Bond with a fresh, accessible feel to invite a broad public audience.
