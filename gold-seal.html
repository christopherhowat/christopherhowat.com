About the project

At Gold Seal Homecare, I helped shape the company's visual output across both design and photography, working to give the brand a more consistent and modern presence across digital channels.

A large part of the work focused on graphic design for campaigns and social content. This included promotional assets, seasonal offers, and branded posts designed to support ongoing marketing activity and strengthen how the company presented itself online.

Alongside design work, I produced photography for the brand, capturing staff, services, and general brand imagery. This helped build a reusable visual library that could be drawn from across campaigns and day-to-day marketing.

I also maintained and developed the company website, making ongoing updates in Webflow to improve layout, structure, and clarity. As part of this, I worked on refining on-page content and SEO elements so that pages were better structured for search visibility and easier for users to navigate.
